Cybersecurity Challenges Facing Colleges

Colleges face unique cybersecurity risks that threaten student safety, research integrity, institutional data, and operational continuity.

Research Data Breaches

  • Intellectual property theft
  • Research findings at risk
  • Federal grant compliance violations
  • International collaboration security concerns

Campus Network Vulnerabilities

  • Open WiFi across campus
  • Legacy system integration
  • Thousands of connected devices
  • Inadequate network segmentation

Educational Technology Risks

  • Learning management system breaches
  • Online platform vulnerabilities
  • Cloud storage security gaps
  • Student device management challenges

Human Factor Vulnerabilities

  • Faculty phishing susceptibility
  • Student security awareness gaps
  • Staff password practices
  • Social engineering attacks targeting research

Financial and Student Data Protection

  • Student financial aid security
  • Tuition payment processing
  • Alumni donor information
  • Health records (medical programs)

Compliance Requirements

  • FERPA compliance mandates
  • State privacy regulations
  • Insurance requirements
  • Accreditation standards
