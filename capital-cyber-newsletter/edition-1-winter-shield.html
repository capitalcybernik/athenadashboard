What is Operation Winter SHIELD?

SHIELD stands for: Securing Homeland Infrastructure by Enhancing Layered Defense

The FBI identified the 10 most critical security actions every organization must take. These are not theory. They are derived from real FBI investigations of breaches that actually happened. Each week of the campaign focuses on one defense tied to a real case.

The FBI's Top 10 Priorities

  1. Adopt phish resistant authentication (security keys, not SMS)
  2. Implement risk based vulnerability management
  3. Track and retire end of life technology
  4. Manage third party vendor risk
  5. Protect security logs with immutable storage
  6. Maintain offline, tested backups
  7. Inventory and protect internet facing systems
  8. Strengthen email authentication
  9. Reduce administrator privileges
  10. Exercise your incident response plan
