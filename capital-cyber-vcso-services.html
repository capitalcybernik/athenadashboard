WHAT IS A VIRTUAL CHIEF SECURITY OFFICER?

A vCSO provides the strategic cybersecurity leadership of a Chief Information Security Officer on a part-time or fractional basis. You get executive-level expertise without the $200,000+ annual salary of a full-time CISO.

A vCSO serves as your:

  • Strategic cybersecurity advisor
  • Compliance program leader
  • Board and executive liaison
  • Incident response coordinator
  • Security team manager
  • Risk management expert

Who Needs a vCSO:

  • Growing businesses without security leadership
  • Companies preparing for compliance (CMMC, HIPAA, etc.)
  • Organizations with security teams that need guidance
  • Businesses facing increasing cyber risks
  • Companies that need board-level security reporting

FAQs

Q: What's the difference between a vCSO and a managed security provider?

An MSSP manages security tools and monitors alerts. A vCSO provides strategic leadership, develops policies, manages compliance programs, and advises executives. Many businesses use both‚Äîa vCSO for strategy and an MSSP for operations.

Q: How does the vCSO interact with our existing IT team?

We work collaboratively with your IT team. The vCSO provides strategic direction, policy guidance, and security oversight while your IT team handles implementation. We can also help develop your IT team's security skills.

Q: Can we transition from vCSO to a full-time CISO later?

Absolutely. Many clients use our vCSO service to build their security program, then hire a full-time CISO once they're ready. We can even help with the hiring process and transition.

Q: What if we have a security incident?

Your vCSO leads the incident response. We coordinate the response team, manage communications, guide remediation, and handle reporting. For clients on Active or Full plans, incident response is included. For Advisory clients, it's available at an hourly rate.

Q: How do you handle confidential information?

We treat your information with the highest confidentiality. All engagements include NDAs, and our team follows strict data handling procedures. We're experienced with classified and sensitive information across defense, healthcare, and financial sectors.
