# Partnership Opportunity Research Report
## Organizations Similar to NextCorps in NYC Metro Area

**Research Date:** February 14, 2026  
**Target Region:** NYC Metro (NYC, Long Island, Westchester, Northern NJ, Western CT)  
**Reference Model:** NextCorps (nextcorps.org) - Rochester, NY innovation & commercialization organization

---

## Executive Summary

This report identifies **10 organizations** in the NYC metro area that closely align with NextCorps' mission of serving manufacturing companies through grants, funding assistance, innovation support, and business growth services. These organizations represent prime partnership opportunities for promoting Cyber Grants Alliance cybersecurity grants to their manufacturing clients.

---

## Rankings by Partnership Potential

### #1 - FUZEHub (New York State MEP)
**Website:** https://fuzehub.com  
**Location:** Albany, NY (Statewide coverage including NYC Metro)  
**Type:** Manufacturing Extension Partnership (MEP) - Statewide Network Hub

**What They Do:**
FuzeHub is the statewide MEP network hub for New York State, serving as the go-to resource for startup, small, and mid-size manufacturers and technology companies. They provide guided access to an extensive network of industry experts, programs, and assets to solve productivity, commercialization, R&D, and growth challenges.

**Grant Programs & Services:**
- Jeff Lawrence Innovation Fund (microelectronics ecosystem support)
- Commercialization Competition
- Innovation Challenge
- Manufacturing Grants program
- Manufacturing Solutions consulting
- Technology commercialization support
- NYS at Work program
- Fund Programs for manufacturers

**Target Manufacturing Sectors:**
- All manufacturing sectors statewide
- Startups and early-stage manufacturers
- Small to medium-sized manufacturers
- Contract manufacturers
- Technology companies

**Why They Would Benefit from Cyber Grants Alliance Partnership:**
FuzeHub already works with manufacturers on innovation funding and has established grant programs. Adding cybersecurity grants to their portfolio would enhance their value proposition to manufacturers who increasingly need cybersecurity compliance (especially for federal contracts). Their statewide reach makes them an ideal distribution partner for cybersecurity grant awareness.

**Decision Maker to Contact:**
- Executive Director or Program Director
- Statewide Network Coordinator
- Contact via: info@fuzehub.com or 518-768-7030
- Contact page: https://fuzehub.com/contact-us/
