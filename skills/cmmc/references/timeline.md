# CMMC 2.0 Implementation Timeline

Final rule (32 CFR Part 170) published October 15, 2024. Effective December 16, 2024.
DFARS acquisition rule (48 CFR Part 204) implements contract requirements.

## Four-Phase Rollout

### Phase 1: November 10, 2025 — November 9, 2026
- DoD includes CMMC Level 1 (Self) or Level 2 (Self) in new solicitations
- Self-assessments accepted for both Level 1 and Level 2
- DoD MAY include Level 2 (C3PAO) for applicable contracts
- Certification must be valid at time of contract award
- May also apply to option period exercises

### Phase 2: November 10, 2026 — November 9, 2027
- Level 2 contracts require C3PAO third-party assessments
- Level 1 self-assessments continue
- DoD MAY include Level 3 (DIBCAC) for applicable contracts
- DoD may delay C3PAO requirement to option period

### Phase 3: November 10, 2027 — November 9, 2028
- Level 2 (C3PAO) expands to all applicable contracts and option periods
- Level 3 (DIBCAC) intended for all applicable contracts
- Supply chain enforcement intensifies
- Limited grace periods

### Phase 4: November 10, 2028+
- Full implementation across all DoD contracts and option periods
- No exceptions for non-compliance
- Ongoing surveillance and recertification
- Supply chain accountability fully enforced

## Certification Validity

| Level | Validity Period | Renewal |
|-------|----------------|---------|
| Level 1 | 1 year | Annual self-assessment + affirmation |
| Level 2 (Self) | 1 year | Annual affirmation |
| Level 2 (C3PAO) | 3 years | Full C3PAO reassessment |
| Level 3 (DIBCAC) | 3 years | Full DIBCAC reassessment |

## Preparation Timeline for Clients

| Timeframe | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Immediate (0-90 days) | Identify FCI/CUI, audit current posture, map network architecture, assign compliance team |
| Short-term (3-6 months) | Gap assessment against NIST 800-171, remediate critical gaps, begin SSP documentation |
| Medium-term (6-12 months) | Implement all required controls, develop POA&M for remaining gaps, schedule C3PAO |
| Ongoing | Continuous monitoring, annual affirmations, workforce training, supply chain verification |

Average preparation time: 12-18 months for Level 2 certification.
