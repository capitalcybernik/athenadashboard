**Prepared for:** Cyber Grants Alliance

---

## Executive Summary

NextCorps, a Rochester-based nonprofit innovation hub and MEP (Manufacturing Extension Partnership) center, provides comprehensive growth services and funding opportunities for manufacturing firms in the Finger Lakes Region and Rochester area. While NextCorps' primary focus is on manufacturing growth, product development, and commercialization, they serve as a critical connector to federal and state funding opportunities, including cybersecurity compliance and CMMC-related grants for manufacturers.

Annette Brenner, Director of Growth Services at NextCorps, specializes in identifying and connecting manufacturing firms with available funding opportunities, including those related to cybersecurity compliance and CMMC implementation.

## About NextCorps

**Organization Type:** Nonprofit innovation hub and MEP center  
**Location:** Sibley Square, Rochester, NY (center of Rochester Innovation Zone)  
**Mission:** Catalyst for entrepreneurship and innovation-based economic development  
**Founded:** Part of the national MEP network under NIST  
**Funding Sources:** NIST, NYSTAR, Empire State Development

### Key Services
- Connect manufacturers to local and national funding resources
- Business improvement project support
- Grant identification and application assistance
- Consulting and advisory services
- Connect firms with vetted consultants and partners

### Eligible Companies
- Rochester and Finger Lakes manufacturers
- Printers and production-related companies
- Food and beverage companies
- Any production-related business in the region

## NextCorps' Role in CMMC Funding

### How They Connect Manufacturers to CMMC Funding
1. **Funding Identification** — Track federal/state grants for cybersecurity compliance
2. **Grant Application Support** — Help with eligibility, documentation, applications
3. **Project Planning & Execution** — Connect with vetted cybersecurity consultants
4. **Consulting & Advisory** — Guidance on CMMC levels, requirements, strategy

## Key Funding Programs

| Program | Sponsor | Amount | Eligibility |
|---|---|---|---|
| NY Manufacturing Growth Grant | ESD/NYSTAR | Varies | NY manufacturers <500 employees |
| NIST MEP Cybersecurity Initiative | NIST | Up to $50K | Small/mid-sized manufacturers |
| DIB Cybersecurity Grant | Federal | Up to $35K | Defense contractors/subs (20-30% cost-share) |
| Finger Lakes Regional Grant | Regional agencies | Varies | Finger Lakes manufacturers |
