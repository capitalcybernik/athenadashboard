# Cyber Grant Summit - Speaker Briefing

**Event Name:** Cyber Grant Summit  
**Event Date:** April 28, 2026  
**Event Time:** 12:00 PM - 1:30 PM EST  
**Format:** Virtual Live Event  
**Platform:** Zoom

---

## Event Overview

The Cyber Grant Summit is a focused 1.5-hour virtual event designed to educate small and medium-sized businesses, manufacturing firms, and nonprofits on how to secure funding for cybersecurity compliance, with a special emphasis on CMMC readiness and practical grant opportunities.

---

## Target Audience

The event will attract a mix of business owners, executives, IT decision-makers, and compliance professionals who are actively seeking practical, actionable advice on how to navigate the complex world of cybersecurity grants and compliance funding.

---

## Your Role as a Speaker

As a speaker at the Cyber Grant Summit, your role is to provide valuable, educational content that empowers our audience to take meaningful action in their compliance journey. Please focus on delivering practical advice, real-world examples, and actionable insights. Avoid sales pitches and instead position your expertise as a resource for attendees.

---

## Session Format & Timing

Each speaker will have exactly **15 minutes** for their presentation. There will be no individual Q&A segments during presentations. Instead, all questions will be consolidated into a final 15-minute Q&A and closing remarks session at the end of the event (1:00 PM - 1:15 PM).

**Timeline:**
- 12:00 PM - 12:15 PM: Rick Dassler - Welcome & The Grant Landscape
- 12:15 PM - 12:30 PM: Annette (NextCorp) - Grants for Manufacturing Firms in NY
- 12:30 PM - 12:45 PM: Nikhil (CMMC Ready Now) - CMMC Ready Now Platform Demo
- 12:45 PM - 1:00 PM: Sam Durso (The Cyber Friend) - Compliance & Controls: A Practical Guide
- 1:00 PM - 1:15 PM: All Speakers - Q&A & Closing Remarks

---

## Speaker-Specific Talking Points

### Rick Dassler - Executive Director, Cyber Grants Alliance

**Session: Welcome & The Grant Landscape (12:00 PM - 12:15 PM)**

- Introduce the Cyber Grants Alliance and its mission to democratize cybersecurity funding
- Highlight the organization's impact: $5M+ in services granted, 1,200+ businesses protected
- Provide a brief overview of available grant programs (Pen Testing, CMMC Tracking Tool, Training, Certifications)
- Explain the importance of CMMC readiness for SMBs and manufacturing firms
- Set the stage for the day's speakers and the value they will provide
- Include a clear call-to-action directing attendees to apply for grants

### Annette K - Funding Specialist, NextCorp

**Session: Grants for Manufacturing Firms in NY (12:15 PM - 12:30 PM)**

- Focus specifically on grant opportunities available to manufacturing firms in New York
- Explain how federal and state grants can be leveraged for CMMC compliance
- Provide 1-2 case studies or examples of successful grant applications from manufacturing firms
- Discuss common barriers manufacturing firms face in achieving compliance and how grants help overcome them
- Highlight NextCorp's role in connecting manufacturers with funding opportunities
- Provide practical next steps for attendees to explore manufacturing-specific grants

### Nikhil B - Co-Founder, CMMC Ready Now

**Session: CMMC Ready Now Platform Demo (12:30 PM - 12:45 PM)**

- Provide a live demonstration of the CMMC Ready Now platform
- Showcase key features: automated compliance tracking, evidence logging, reporting tools
- Explain how the platform helps organizations manage and track compliance controls
- Demonstrate how the platform simplifies the audit-readiness process
- Highlight the time and cost savings organizations achieve with the platform
- Discuss how the platform integrates with grant programs to accelerate compliance
- Include a brief overview of support and training resources available

### Sam Durso - Cybersecurity Consultant, The Cyber Friend

**Session: Compliance & Controls: A Practical Guide (12:45 PM - 1:00 PM)**

- Provide a practical, accessible guide to understanding cybersecurity compliance controls
- Use real-world examples and case studies to illustrate compliance concepts
- Share best practices for implementing controls in resource-constrained environments
- Discuss common compliance mistakes and how to avoid them
- Offer actionable advice that attendees can implement immediately
- Address the human element of compliance (training, awareness, culture)
- Emphasize that compliance is achievable for SMBs with the right resources and support
