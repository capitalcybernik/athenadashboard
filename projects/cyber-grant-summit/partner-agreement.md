# Cyber Grant Summit - Event Partner Agreement

**Effective Date:** April 28, 2026

This Agreement is made and entered into as of the date of signature below, by and between the **Cyber Grants Alliance** (hereinafter referred to as "CGA"), and **[Partner Name]** (hereinafter referred to as "Partner").

---

## 1. Scope of Partnership

The Partner agrees to participate as a speaker at the **"Cyber Grant Summit"** virtual event (hereinafter referred to as the "Event"), scheduled for **April 28, 2026, from 12:00 PM to 1:30 PM EST**.

Partner will deliver a **15-minute presentation** on the topic of **[Session Topic]**. All questions from attendees will be consolidated into a single Q&A session at the end of the event (1:00 PM - 1:15 PM EST), during which the Partner may be asked to respond to audience questions.

**Event Details:**
- **Event Name:** Cyber Grant Summit
- **Date:** April 28, 2026
- **Time:** 12:00 PM - 1:30 PM EST
- **Format:** Virtual Live Event (Zoom)
- **Expected Attendance:** 150-200 participants
- **Audience:** SMB owners, executives, IT decision-makers, and compliance professionals

---

## 2. Lead Management and Ownership

The Cyber Grants Alliance is the sole owner of the attendee list for the Event. All attendee information, including names, email addresses, and company information, is the exclusive property of CGA.

**Distribution of Attendee Information:**

A list of attendees who have explicitly opted-in to receive communications from partners will be provided to Event sponsors only. Speakers who are not sponsors will not receive direct access to the full attendee list. However, attendees who specifically request to connect with a speaker will have their contact information shared with that speaker.

**Lead Qualification:**

CGA will manage the initial qualification and routing of leads. All inquiries from attendees regarding a Partner's services or offerings must be directed through CGA for initial contact and qualification.

---

## 3. Non-Compete and Exclusivity

For a period of **ninety (90) days** following the Event, the Partner agrees to the following:

**Lead Funneling:** All leads generated from the Event (including attendee inquiries, follow-up requests, and business opportunities) must be funneled through CGA. The Partner agrees not to directly solicit or independently engage with Event attendees without prior coordination with CGA.

**Exclusivity:** The Partner agrees not to directly solicit or engage with Event attendees for services that overlap with or are similar to those offered by CGA or other Event partners without the prior written consent of CGA.

**Prohibited Activities:** The Partner shall not:
- Directly contact Event attendees outside of CGA-facilitated introductions
- Offer competing services or products to Event attendees
- Encourage attendees to bypass CGA for lead management
- Share attendee information with third parties

**Permitted Activities:** The Partner may:
- Respond to attendee inquiries that come through CGA
- Follow up with attendees who have explicitly requested contact
- Share general educational content or resources with attendees who have opted-in
- Participate in post-event communications coordinated by CGA

---

## 4. Confidentiality

The Partner agrees to keep confidential all non-public information disclosed by CGA, including but not limited to:
- The attendee list and attendee information
- Proprietary information shared during Event planning
- Financial terms of other partnerships
- Internal CGA strategies and processes
- Any other information marked as confidential

This obligation of confidentiality shall survive the termination of this Agreement and continue for a period of two (2) years after the Event.

---

## 5. Intellectual Property and Branding

**Presentation Materials:** The Partner retains ownership of their presentation materials and slides. CGA may use the presentation materials for post-event distribution to attendees and for promotional purposes, provided that proper attribution is given.

**Branding:** The Partner agrees to maintain consistency with CGA branding guidelines during the Event. CGA may use the Partner's name, company name, and logo in promotional materials for the Event.

**Recording:** The Event will be recorded and the recording may be shared with registered attendees and used for promotional purposes. The Partner agrees to be recorded and understands that the recording may be distributed.

---

## 6. Representations and Warranties

The Partner represents and warrants that:
- The Partner has the authority to enter into this Agreement
- The Partner's presentation will be original, accurate, and not infringe on any third-party rights
- The Partner will not make any false or misleading claims during the Event
- The Partner will comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- The Partner will not use the Event for unauthorized marketing or solicitation

---

## 7. Limitation of Liability

Neither CGA nor the Partner shall be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages arising out of or in connection with this Agreement or the Event.

---

## 8. Term and Termination

**Term:** This Agreement shall commence on the date first signed below and shall continue in full force and effect until the completion of the 90-day exclusivity period following the Event (approximately July 27, 2026).

**Termination:** This Agreement may be terminated by either party with 30 days written notice. In the event of termination, the Partner's obligations regarding lead management and exclusivity shall remain in effect for the duration of the 90-day period.

**Termination for Cause:** CGA may immediately terminate this Agreement if the Partner:
- Violates the non-compete or exclusivity provisions
- Breaches the confidentiality obligations
- Engages in fraudulent or deceptive practices
- Fails to deliver their presentation as agreed

---

## 9. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved through good-faith negotiation between the parties. If negotiation fails, disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration under the rules of the American Arbitration Association.
