# Federal Technology Initiative Grant (TIG) — CY2026

## ⚠️ KEY FINDING: LIMITED ELIGIBILITY
This grant is administered by the **Legal Services Corporation (LSC)**, NOT a general cybersecurity grant program.

**Eligibility:** Applicants **must be current grantees** of LSC Basic Field-General, Basic Field-Migrant, or Basic Field-Native American grants. This is NOT open to the general public or cybersecurity firms.

**CGA Relevance:** Low, unless CGA partners with an existing LSC grantee to provide cybersecurity technology services to legal aid organizations.

## Grant Details

**Agency:** Legal Services Corporation (LSC)
**Program:** Technology Initiative Grant (TIG)
**Purpose:** Fund creative/innovative uses of technology to enhance legal services delivery to low-income populations

### Grant Types
1. **General TIG** — New technology projects
2. **Sustainability, Enhancement, and Adoption (SEA)** — For orgs nearing completion or recently completed a TIG (last 3 years)
3. **TIG Planning Grant** — Planning phase funding
4. **Technology Improvement Projects** — Tech capacity building

### Key Deadlines
| Milestone | Date |
|-----------|------|
| Pre-Applications (General TIG + SEA) | **April 10, 2026** (11:59 PM ET) |
| Technology Improvement Project apps | **May 22, 2026** (11:59 PM ET) |
| General TIG, SEA, Planning Grant apps | **June 30, 2026** (11:59 PM ET) |
| LSC Info Meeting for invited applicants | **May 21, 2026** (2:00 PM EDT) |

### Funding
- SEA grants: minimum $40,000 recommended, 24-month terms
- General TIG: amounts vary

### How to Apply
- All submissions via LSC's GrantEase system: http://lscgrants.lsc.gov
- Must be existing LSC grantee
